Basic Info

  • Title
  • Their Eyes Were Watching God
  • Author
  • Zora Neale Hurston
  • Published
  • First published in 1937 by J. B. Lippincott, Inc.
  • Genre
  • Bildungsroman
  • Setting
  • The novel is set sometime during the 1920's or 30's in a rural area in Florida.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is told in the third person point of view from a narrator. Due to the narrators details and descriptions it seems to be as if Janie, the protagonist, is the one telling the story in the third person.
  • Conflict
  • Janie seeks to define herself as a woman and spiritually during a time when it is difficult for women to succeed, especially black women like Janie.
